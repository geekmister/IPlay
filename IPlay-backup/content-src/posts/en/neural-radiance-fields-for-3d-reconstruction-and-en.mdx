# Neural Radiance Fields for 3D Reconstruction and Improvements

> Perspective: The main bottleneck in NeRF is training efficiency and reconstruction latency.

## Research Question

This article focuses on NeRF reconstruction: improving interpretability, stability, and deployability while preserving strong performance.

## Method Perspective

1. Define task constraints before increasing model complexity.
2. Use both perceptual and objective metrics for evaluation.
3. Replay failure cases during training to reduce tail-risk.

## Evaluation Suggestions

- Report not only peak scores, but also variance and worst-case behavior.
- Add cross-domain validation to avoid single-dataset overfitting.
- Include latency and memory costs for engineering decisions.

## Representative Papers and Links

- [OpenMax (2016)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1511.06233)
- [NTS-Net (2018)](https://arxiv.org/abs/1809.00287)
- [LLaVA (2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.08485)

## Production Insight

The main bottleneck in NeRF is training efficiency and reconstruction latency. In practical delivery, I strongly recommend using a minimum loop of failure replay, metric dashboarding, and rollback plans.
